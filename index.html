How It Works

Three simple steps from insight to execution

1. Discover Markets

Browse thousands of binary and scalar markets across politics, finance, technology, and global events. Each market represents a specific outcome with Yes/No trading.

2. Trade Positions

Buy "Yes" if you believe an event will occur, "No" if you don't. Prices reflect market consensus in real-time. Trade with as little as $1 per contract.

3. Settle & Profit

When the event resolves, contracts settle at $1 for correct predictions, $0 for incorrect. Oracle-based resolution ensures fair, transparent outcomes.
